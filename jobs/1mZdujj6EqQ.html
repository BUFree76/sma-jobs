Tom Hollandsworth – State Farm Agency is hiring a full-time Entry-Level Insurance Sales Representative to join our team. This role is ideal for motivated individuals looking to start a sales career in a professional, goal-driven environment. Responsibilities include generating insurance quotes, conducting consultative sales conversations, closing new business, following up with leads, building strong relationships with new and existing clients, cross-selling additional coverage, and providing prompt, accurate customer service. Applicants must be able to obtain a Property and Casualty license and a Life and Health license. Sales, retail, hospitality, or other customer-facing experience is preferred but not required. We provide training and guidance throughout the licensing process. This position is with a State Farm independent contractor agent. Employees of State Farm agents are not employees of State Farm Insurance Companies.
