Health Insurance Sales Agent Mark Bartley State Farm Agency – JONESBORO, GA Job Type: Full-Time Compensation: $45,000 – $65,000 + Commission (DOE) About the Role As a Health Insurance Sales Agent with Mark Bartley State Farm Agency, you’ll build trusted relationships with clients, educate them on health insurance options, and help individuals and families find the right coverage to fit their needs. This role combines consultative sales with excellent customer service and a focus on long-term client satisfaction. Key Responsibilities Generate and follow up on leads to build a strong book of business. Develop client relationships and assess individual health insurance needs. Explain plan options, benefits, and coverage details in clear, customer-focused terms. Prepare and present insurance quotes and enroll clients in appropriate health insurance plans. Maintain accurate client records and policy documentation. Provide ongoing service to clients, including renewals and policy changes. What We’re Looking For Active Life & Health Insurance License in Georgia. Strong communication and interpersonal skills. Customer-focused attitude with a consultative sales approach. Self-motivated with a drive to build relationships and close sales. Preferred Qualifications Prior experience in health or life insurance sales. Familiarity with quoting and enrollment systems. Why Join Us Competitive base salary plus uncapped commission potential. Supportive team environment with ongoing training. Opportunity to grow your career and client base.
