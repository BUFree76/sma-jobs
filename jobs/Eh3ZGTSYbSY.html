About This Role

We are seeking a dynamic and customer-focused professional to join our team. This role involves engaging with clients to understand their insurance needs, providing tailored solutions, and building lasting relationships. As an Insurance Agent, you will play a vital role in expanding our client base and ensuring exceptional service delivery across various insurance products. The ideal candidate will possess strong communication skills, sales expertise, and a passion for helping clients protect their future. Bilingual abilities and experience in outside sales or retail sales are highly valued. Familiarity with sales administration, marketing strategies, and benefits administration processes. Exceptional customer service skills with a professional demeanor.
